Iranian and Afghan refugees protest on the schlossplatz, Karlsruhe in Germany

a number of asylum seekers, most of them Afghans and Iranians, They were protesting for asylum application problems in Germany. The asylum seekers wrote their demands on paper sheets and took pictures of the crimes of the Taleban and Islamic Republic of Iran among the crowds in this city, they showed that there is no humanity in the Islamic Republic of Iran and the Islamic state of Afghanistan, in this regard protest spokesman Teymour Amjadi stated that this is our first protest, I hope that the refugee issues will return to their normal and legal procedures and give a positive answer to our asylum seekers who have fled from execution, murder and imprisonment Otherwise, I declare from this platform that we will continue to protest and we will have bigger ones ahead of us.
